My Background
My journey from fashion design to UI/UX was a natural transition, where my expertise in visual storytelling and aesthetics paved the way for digital design. What excites me about UI/UX is its fusion of beauty and functionality, creating immersive experiences that resonate with users. Rooted in fashion, I bring a deep understanding of visual communication to my design approach.

Driven by a passion for leveraging technology to enhance human experiences, I'm fascinated by the intersection of art and technology in solving real-world problems. My nomadic lifestyle has broadened my perspective, fueling curiosity and a willingness to explore new ideas.

My strength lies in blending artistic creativity with user-centered design principles, always eager to learn and adapt in this rapidly evolving field. While I'm relatively new to UI/UX, my enthusiasm and dedication compensate for any lack of experience.

I'm drawn to projects that challenge conventions, whether collaborating with startups or established companies. Excited to contribute and grow in the world of UI/UX design, I'm committed to making a meaningful impact.
